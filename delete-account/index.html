Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Macaw AI account and associated data.

Delete inside the app

Sign in, open Settings, select Delete account, and confirm. This is the fastest way to delete your account.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app, contact macawaiapp@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Macaw AI account.

Data that is deleted

Your authentication account, profile, generated-logo records, and stored logo files are deleted. Your remaining token balance is also removed.

Data that may be retained

Limited support, transaction, security, legal, and non-reversible abuse-prevention records may be retained when reasonably necessary. A hashed identity record may be retained only to prevent repeated starter-token claims.
