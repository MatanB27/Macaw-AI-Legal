Macaw AI

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Macaw AI handles information when you use the app.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Information we collect

Macaw AI collects account information needed to provide the service, including your email address, authentication identifier, token balance, generated-logo records, purchase records, and support requests.

Prompts and generated logos

When you generate a logo, we process the prompt and settings you provide. Generated images, prompts, settings, and related metadata may be stored so you can access and manage your logo history.

Payments

Token purchases are processed by the app stores and RevenueCat. We do not store full payment card details. We store purchase events and token transaction records needed to credit your account and support purchase history.

How we use information

We use information to authenticate users, generate and store logos, manage token balances and purchase history, provide support, prevent abuse, maintain security, and improve reliability.

Service providers

Macaw AI uses Supabase for authentication, database, and file storage; fal.ai for AI image generation; RevenueCat and app-store services for purchases; and Google or Apple for supported social sign-in.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared with service providers only as needed to operate Macaw AI, comply with law, protect users, or prevent fraud and abuse.

Retention

Account data, prompts, and generated-logo records are retained while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Support, transaction, and security records may be retained to resolve issues, prevent abuse, meet payment obligations, or comply with law.

Account deletion

You can delete your account inside the app. Deletion removes your account, profile, generated-logo records, and stored logo files, except limited information retained for security, fraud prevention, payment compliance, or legal obligations.

Abuse prevention

To prevent repeated free-token claims, Macaw AI may retain a non-reversible hashed record of an account identity after deletion. This is used only to determine whether starter tokens were previously claimed.

AI model training

Macaw AI does not use your prompts or generated logos to train its own AI models. Third-party AI providers may process submitted content under their own service and privacy terms.

Your choices

You may access saved content in the app, delete individual logos, delete your account, or contact support with a privacy request.
