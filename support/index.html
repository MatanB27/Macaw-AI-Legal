Support information for Macaw AI.

Contact support

Email macawaiapp@gmail.com for help with Macaw AI. Include the email address on your Macaw AI account and a description of the issue. Do not send passwords or authentication codes.

Open a support ticket

You can also sign in to Macaw AI, open Settings, and select Support to submit a ticket.

Privacy and account requests

For privacy questions, email macawaiapp@gmail.com. To permanently delete your account, use the in-app deletion control or follow the account-deletion instructions.
